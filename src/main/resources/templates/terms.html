Platform Guidelines

Terms of Use

These terms describe the expected use of D3FENDer as a defensive security assessment platform.

1. Acceptance of Terms

By using D3FENDer, you agree to use the platform responsibly and only for lawful defensive security purposes.

2. Authorized Use

You may only assess systems, software, SBOM files, or environments that you own or have explicit permission to evaluate.

3. Prohibited Activity

D3FENDer must not be used for unauthorized testing, exploitation, credential theft, harmful automation, or attacks against third-party systems.

4. Security Recommendations

Generated results are intended to support decision-making. Users remain responsible for validating findings before applying changes in real environments.

5. Accounts and Access

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials and for activity performed through their account.

6. Service Changes

D3FENDer may evolve over time as features, pricing, assessment methods, or integrations are improved.
